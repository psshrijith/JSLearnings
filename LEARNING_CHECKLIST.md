## 🎯 Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **One topic at a time.** Don't jump ahead.
2. **Always implement.** No skipping code.
3. **Write without looking** after you've learned it.
4. **Solve problems immediately** after each topic.
5. **Revise every weekend** — no exceptions.
6. **Exit criteria must pass** before the next week begins.
